Michele Malooley is a leading authority in the Medicare insurance industry. Michele's success is founded on over 30 years of professional experience as a solely owned independent agency located in Palm Beach County. Her company's mission is simple: to provide clients the tools and education necessary to navigate a highly technical insurance environment and make informed decisions.

Michele is the Past President of the PBAHU (now known as NABIP) and has served on the Board since 2012, representing her industry at the state and national levels and advocating in Washington D.C. and Tallahassee for effective legislation. She is a Sitting Member of the Medicare Advisory Group Subcommittee and a Certified Senior Advisor (CSA) since 2003.

Michele is a graduate of Palm Beach State College and began her career working alongside her parents in their independent insurance agency. She and her husband, William, are residents of Palm Beach County. Michele is committed to helping you plan today for the peace of mind you need tomorrow.

Specialties

Michele offers CONFIDENCE and CLARITY over CONFUSION when it comes to your insurance options.

Medicare

Medicare can be very confusing, as Medicare laws and plans change frequently. Michele provides stress-free, professional, confidential, and easy-to-understand Medicare education and planning at no cost to you. With Michele, you'll have an "Agent for Life."

Life Insurance

Investing in the right policy now can help ensure your family's financial stability for years to come. Michele can help you choose from term life, whole life, accidental death benefits, universal life, and final expense policies.

Long Term Care Insurance

If you are diagnosed with a chronic illness or return home from a rehab center, a long-term care policy provides benefits including assisted living support and a significant decrease in expense costs.

Dental and Vision

With dental and vision policies, you and your family can make convenient appointments, reduce your bills, and access the best specialists in your area.

Critical Illness Insurance

Becoming seriously ill can have a huge impact on your finances. Critical illness and cancer coverage provide cash benefits to help cover bills and expenses your current health insurance won't pay for.

Annuities

Michele can help you evaluate annuity options designed to provide steady, reliable income as part of your long-term financial and retirement plan.
